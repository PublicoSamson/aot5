Attack on Titan

About the Anime

Attack on Titan (Japanese: 進撃の巨人, Hepburn: Shingeki no Kyojin; lit. 'The Advancing Giant') is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. Set in a world where humanity is forced to live in cities surrounded by three enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans, the story follows Eren Yeager, an adolescent boy who vows to exterminate the Titans after they bring about the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother.

The anime was produced by Wit Studio (seasons 1-3) and MAPPA (season 4). Key broadcast milestones include April to September 2013 for Season 1, April to June 2017 for Season 2, and a massive final season spanning from December 2020 all the way to the grand finale in November 2023.

Attack on Titan - Eren Jaeger

Eren Jaeger

Introduction

Eren Jaeger was a former member of the Scout Regiment and the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. He was the only son of Grisha and Carla Jaeger, and the last holder of the Attack Titan, Founding Titan, and the War Hammer Titan.

Born in Shiganshina District, Eren's life changed forever when the Colossal and Armored Titans breached Wall Maria. After witnessing the death of his mother, Eren swore an oath to destroy every single Titan. His journey led him from a vengeful soldier to a figure who would change the fate of the entire world.

Attack on Titan - Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman

Introduction

Mikasa Ackerman is one of the two deuteragonists of the series, along with Armin Arlert. After her parents were murdered, Mikasa was rescued by Eren Yeager and lived with his family before the fall of Wall Maria.

She is the last descendant of the Shogun clan on Paradis Island and holds significant political power in Hizuru. Though she desires only a peaceful life, she joined the military and became the top soldier of the 104th Training Corps, serving as one of the Survey Corps' greatest assets.

Attack on Titan - Armin Arlert

Armin Arlert

Introduction

Armin Arlert is the 15th commander of the Survey Corps and a childhood friend of Eren Jaeger and Mikasa Ackerman. Although he was physically among the weakest of the 104th Training Corps, his intelligence and strategic genius proved to be an invaluable asset.

Armin always dreamed of seeing the outside world, specifically the "Sea" described in his grandfather's book. After the Battle of Shiganshina, he became the successor to the Colossal Titan, gaining the power of one of the Nine Titans.

Attack on Titan - Levi Ackerman

Levi Ackerman

Introduction

Levi Ackerman, often formally referred to as Captain Levi, is the squad leader of the Special Operations Squad within the Survey Corps and is widely known as humanity's strongest soldier. Despite his short stature and blunt personality, his skills in combat are unmatched.

Raised in the Underground by Kenny the Ripper, Levi learned to survive in the harshest conditions before being recruited into the Scouts by Erwin Smith. He is known for his obsession with cleanliness, his cold exterior, and his deep, unspoken loyalty to his comrades.
